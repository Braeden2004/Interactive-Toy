---Chicken Fight----
by: Braeden Hammill


----About----

The purpose of this game is to live the fantasy of beating up the infamous chicken from Family Guy
as the one and only Peter Griffin. There are no points or objectives, just have fun beating up the chicken.

Pickup the magical pajamas to gain a power up that increases Peters power.

----Controls-----

Move left and right with the arrow keys, and attack with spacebar.

press [R] to restart.
